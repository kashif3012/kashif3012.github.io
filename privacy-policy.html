Privacy Policy for Imaanix

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Imaanix ("we", "our", or "us") provides Islamic quote and status content through the Imaanix mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

Imaanix does not require account signup and we do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Imaanix stores limited app data locally on your device, including:

  • Favorite quote IDs
  • Cached quote data for offline use and faster loading
  • Quote content version and last sync date

This information remains on your device and is used only to support app functionality.

3. Internet Access and Remote Content

The app may fetch quote or version updates from a remote source over the internet to keep content current. Standard technical request data such as IP address and device user-agent may be processed by network providers or hosting infrastructure as part of normal internet communication.

4. Permissions Used

  • Internet - Used to load quotes and advertisements.
  • Storage / Media - Used when users download generated quote images.

5. Sharing and Export

When you use the save or share features, the app generates an image on your device. If you choose to share it, the content is passed to the selected application through your device's share functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

Imaanix uses third-party services to support subscriptions and advertising within the app.

The app uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and entitlement restoration. RevenueCat may process subscription-related technical data such as app user identifiers, purchase tokens/transaction metadata, product identifiers, and diagnostics needed to validate purchases and restore access across reinstalls.

Learn more about RevenueCat's privacy practices here: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain information including:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • App interaction data

This information is used to provide relevant advertisements and improve advertising services.

Learn more about Google's privacy practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect app data. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Imaanix is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

9. Your Choices

  • You can clear app data from device settings to remove cached or favorite data.
  • You can stop content updates by using the app offline.
  • You control if and where generated images are shared.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will appear on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, contact: imaanix.app@gmail.com
